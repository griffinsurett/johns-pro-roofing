## Repairs for flat and low-slope roofs

Most commercial leaks come from a handful of failure points, and finding the real one is half the job:

- **Membrane punctures and tears** in TPO, EPDM, and modified bitumen
- **Failed seams and laps** where sections meet
- **Flashing and detail leaks** at curbs, drains, HVAC units, and parapet walls
- **Ponding water** and drainage issues that shorten roof life
- **Coating and resealing** to extend the system you already have
