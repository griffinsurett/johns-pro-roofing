## Why flat roofs need a specialist

On a sloped roof, gravity moves water off. On a flat roof, water sits — so seams, flashing, drainage, and detailing have to be done right or the roof leaks. We install to manufacturer spec, get the slope-to-drain right, and seal every penetration properly so your roof actually performs through New Jersey's freeze-thaw winters and heavy summer rain.
