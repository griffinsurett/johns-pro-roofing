## When it's time to replace your roof

Most asphalt-shingle roofs in New Jersey last 20–25 years, but our weather speeds that up — nor'easters, high winds, ice dams, and summer heat all take their toll. If your roof is aging, curling or losing shingles, showing daylight in the attic, or driving up energy bills, a full replacement usually costs less over time than chasing one repair after another. Not sure which you need? Start with a [roof inspection](/roofing/roof-inspections) and we'll give you an honest answer.
