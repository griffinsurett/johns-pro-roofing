## When to get a roof inspection

- **After a storm** — wind, hail, and falling limbs cause damage you can't see from the ground
- **Before buying or selling a home** — know what you're getting into
- **When you spot warning signs** — interior stains, missing shingles, granules in the gutters, daylight in the attic
- **For an aging roof** — to plan ahead instead of waiting for a leak
- **For an insurance claim** — we document the damage clearly to support your case
