## What to do while you wait for us

1. **Move what you can** out from under the leak and put down a bucket or tub.
2. **Catch and relieve pooling water** in the ceiling — a small hole in a sagging bulge drains it safely instead of letting it collapse.
3. **Stay off the roof.** Wet roofs are dangerous, and storm damage can hide weak spots. Let our crew handle it.
4. **Take photos** of the damage inside and out — it helps with any insurance claim.
